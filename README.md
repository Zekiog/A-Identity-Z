## What is A-Identity-Z?

A-Identity-Z is a multilingual AI services operation focused on **quality-assured translation, interpretation, and AI-assisted localization** across 7 languages.

We combine native human language expertise with AI workflow tooling to deliver outputs that are:
- **Accurate** — native-level review at every step
- **Consistent** — terminology management and style guides per client
- **Scalable** — AI-assisted pipelines with human oversight gates
- **Auditable** — every output traceable to source and reviewer
